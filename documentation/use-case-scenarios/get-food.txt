Get Food

Context:
Cook is logged in
Cook is on the view inventory page

Primary Flow:

1. Cook selects a food item from the list
2. Cook requests to retrieve food from item on the list
3. System prompts the cook to enter the quantity from the selected food they would like to take
4. Cook enters a valid quantity and submits request
5. System subtracts the quantity taken from the food item on the list, removing the food item
from the list entirely if all quantity was taken

Alternative Flows:

There are no food items left in list
A1. The button to request to retrieve food is disabled

No food item in the list is selected
A1. The button to request to retrieve food is disabled

Cook requests higher quantity than exists in food item
A4. Cook enters a quantity higher than available
A5. System notifies the cook that the requested quantity is too high, and shows the max available
A6. Cook acknowledges the message
A7. Return to primary flow step 3

Cook provides no input for quantity
A4. Cook does not enter a quantity or leaves the field blank
A5. Submit button is disabled until cook provides input

Cook provides invalid input
A4. Cook enters invalid input and presses submit
A5. System notifies the cook that the submitted value is invalid, with appropriate message
A6. Return to primary flow step 3
