View Time Until Expiration

Context:
Cook is logged in
Cook is on the view inventory page

Primary Flow:

1. Cook requests to sort the food items in order of soonest to expire
2. System presents cook with reordered list, showing food items soonest to expire first
3. Cook views list of food items

Alternative Flows:

There are less than 2 perishable food items in the list
A1. The option to sort the food items in order of soonest to expire is disabled.
